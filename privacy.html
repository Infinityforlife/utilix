Utilix - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7/20/2025

Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data Utilix collects, how it's used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • User IDs and Guild IDs: For basic functionality, logs, or command usage.
  • Message Content: Only when voluntarily submitted (e.g. tickets or reports).
  • Scammer Reports: User-submitted data including Discord IDs and reasons.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide Bot functionality (e.g. managing tickets, handling reports).
  • To maintain records for scammer reports (to assist in community moderation).
  • To monitor abuse and maintain service quality.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized Bot developers or maintainers. Ticket and report data may be retained until manually deleted by you or the Bot admins.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data may be shared with Discord upon legal request or to enforce community safety.

5. User Rights

You may request data removal by contacting the Bot developer via the support server or email. Server admins may opt-out of the scammer database feature via settings if supported.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the Bot means you accept the latest version.

7. Cookies and Analytics

Utilix does not use cookies. However, basic usage analytics (e.g. command frequency, uptime data) may be collected to improve Bot functionality. This data is anonymized and not linked to individual users.

8. Third-Party Services

If Utilix integrates with any third-party services (e.g., moderation APIs, uptime monitors), only necessary data is shared, and only when required for core Bot functionality. These services are required to follow similar privacy and security standards.
